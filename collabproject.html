[Pro19A] Information Hiding using Image Enhancement

Project Description:
In this project, we hide information while we are performing image enhancement on the image. In most of my recent IH works, I am focusing on proposing new techniques which integrate IH with some common image processing operations, particularly those daily operations. You may refer to my motivations of all these proposals in the blog posts. To verify the feasibility of the proposal (IH in image enhancement), we started it with median filtering. We studied simple (and typical) median filtering technique, and redesign it to carry data (i.e., binary bit of information). In paper [Pro19A-1], we perform series of experiments to verify the performance of our very first prototype (developed in MATLAB), by measuring its embedding capacity, image quality, basic robustness in various parameter settings. It is proven that our idea (IH in image enhancement) is feasible and we are working on a more complex algorithm in realizing the ideas and to test further in this advanced prototype.
